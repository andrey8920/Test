Nicole's story

Science tells us that good nutrition can make a big difference to the way we feel. Even small changes can make a big difference.

The changes that I made six years ago when I established The Good Nutrition Co. made a big difference to how I helped people with their health issues.

Through many years of working in chronic disease management within the pharmaceutical industry, I saw how the health of our nation was rapidly deteriorating. I also experienced first hand the challenges of maintaining my own health while working within a stressful corporate environment.
